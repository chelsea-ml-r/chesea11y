CPACC Preparation and Resources

100 Hours of A11Y

I spent about one hundred hours studying to become a Certified Professional in Accessibility Core Competencies with the International Association of Accessibility Professionals from November 12 of 2024 to February 6 of 2025.

This section of my website, which houses over fifty thousand words of notes, quizzes, reflections, and mnemonics will hopefully be useful to you, a prospective test-taker.

On March 31, 2025, I received my results: I scored above average on all three domains, and my scaled score was 775 out of 800.

Please see the disclaimers I have before utilizing any of the resources on this site.

Information

  • Final Reflection. I reflect on the exam and the motivation, confidence, and heartbreak the preparation experience brought me. I also critique the Body of Knowledge.
  • My Study Method. Includes a list of facts that I memorized, resources I utilized, notes on the methodology used in constructing this blog, and a readiness assessment
  • Logistical Details. I explain in detail the process of registering and taking the exam. This is the article I wish I had as a person I with mega testing anxiety.

Tools

  • Flashcard Game. This has 34 multiple choice questions that I've written out by hand.
  • Demographics Game. This tests all of the figures listed in the Body of Knowledge in an interactive multiple-choice format.
  • All Legislation. As it says on the tin, all legislation from Domain 3 listed on a single page. There's pretty colours, and the information is presented in different ways to aid memorization!
