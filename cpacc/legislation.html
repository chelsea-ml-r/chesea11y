Legislation Compilation

General Kinds of Accessibility Legislation

Reasonable Accommodation

Legislation that follow the 'reasonable accommodation' approach basically don't require that an environment is accessible, until the very moment that someone needs it to be. There is an obligation to accommodate that person who then needs accessibility.

An example of this would be the EU Employment Directive.

Universal Accessibility

As opposed to legislation that takes on the 'accommodation' approach, legislation like Section 508 mandate that services are accessible by default, regardless if a Disabled person has come into a barier.

Anti-discrimination Law

The goal of antidiscrimination law is to ensure that people are not discriminated against for a certain trait. Forms of discimination may include harassment, or an inaccessible environment, on top of traditional discrimination like outright denying a person a job or opportunity or entrance into a building because they hold some kind of trait.

Procurement Law

Laws that cover accessibility requirements for services or products that are contracted from a public body.

Domain Specific Law

Laws pertaining to certain domains where accessibility is relevant.

Accessibility Standards

These are government standards used to determine what is and isn't accessible. For example, EN 301 549 is a ICT standard laying out what counts as accessible and not, and is then referred to by both the European Accessibility Act (covering private sector) and the European Web Accessibility Directive (public sector). I've come across some sources that erroneously say that EN 301 549 itself is a procurement law. It is not, it's a technical standard.

Names, Dates, Jurisdictions

Universal Declaration of Human Rights

1948

UN

Grant human rights.

European Convention on Human Rights

1950

Council of Europe, European Court of Human Rights

Grant rights (civil and political)

European Social Charter

1961

Council of Europe, European Court of Human Rights

Grant rights (economic and social)

Declaration on the Rights of Disabled Persons

1975

UN

Right to rehabilitation, explicit acknowledgment of disability as protected characteristic. No enforcement.

African Charter on Human and Peoples' Rights

1981

Organization of African Unity

Grants human rights. Disability status not named.

European Social Charter Additional Protocol

1988

Council of Europe, European Court of Human Rights

Elaboration of social charter.

Revised Charter (European Social Charter)

1996

Council of Europe, European Court of Human Rights

Elaboration of social charter.

Inter-American Convention on the Elimination of All Forms of Discrimination Against Persons with Disabilities

1999

Organization of American States

Call for environmental accessibility, rehabilitation, and Elimination of All Forms of Discrimination.

EU Charter of Fundamental Rights

2000

EU, Court Justice of the EU

Collates civil, economic, social rights within EU framework.

Arab Charter on Human Rights

2004

League of Arab States

Affirms human rights, disability status named. Article 40 talks about accessibility, education, health, community living for Disabled people.

Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities

2006

UN

Legal instrument, makes explicit all steps that must be taken in order for Disabled people to enjoy human rights.

ASEAN Human Rights Declaration

2012

Association of Southeast Asian Nations

Grants human rights. Disability named, doubled down on as 'vulnerable population.'

Incheon Strategy

2012

ESCAP

Strategy for CRPD in Asia. Accessibility is one of ten goals.

Treaty of Marrakesh

2013

World Intellectual Property Organization

Copyright exception for production of accessible texts, cross-border exchange of materials.

African Disability Rights Protocol

2018

Organization of African Unity

Adapts elements of CRPD, adapted to regional context.

Americans with Disabilities Act

1990

US

Affirms equal opportunity, nondiscrimination in public spaces and public services, accessible telecommunications.

Ontarians with Disabilities

2001

Ontario

Non-discrimination, public services to be accessible, no teeth.

Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act

2005

Ontario

Accessibility in public, private, this time with teeth.

Employment Equality Directive

2000

EU

Requires reasonable accommodation in employment.

The Equality Act

2010

UK

Catch-all anti-discrimination in all public and private spheres, reasonable accommodation.

Cypriot Persons with Disabilities Act

2000

Cyprus

Kinda like the ADA, but in Cyprus.

Loi pour l'égalité des droits et des chances, la participation et la citoyenneté des personnes handicapées

2005

France

Accessibility for all facilities open to the public, criminal charges for those that do not comply.

From Patient to Citizen Action Plan

2000

Sweden

Action plan that pre-empted anti-discrimination and accessibility laws.

Act On The Prohibition Of Discrimination Against Persons With Disabilities And Remedy Against Infringement Of Their Rights

2017

Korea

Anti-discrimination based on reasonable accommodation.

Services and Supports for Persons with Disabilities Act

2005

Japan

Social funding for rehabilitation and support services.

Employment Promotion Act for Persons with Disabilities

2012

Japan

Labour quota, provisions for labout rehabilitational support.

Act on the Elimination of Discrimination against Persons with Disabilities

2013

Japan

Ratifies the UNCRPD.

Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act

2016

India

Ratifies the CRPD, criminalizes those who humiliate Disabled persons.

21st Century Communications and Video Accessibility Act (CVAA)

2010

US Federal Communications Commission

Modernizes accessible communications and video accessibility, addresses accessible products, and accessible television programming.

Air Carrier Access Act

1986

US Department of Transportation

Non-discrimination in aircraft, requires accessible services including ICT.

Air Carrier Access Amendments Act (Proposed)

2017 dead

US

Stricter penalties and enforcement of the ACAA.

Audiovisual Media Services Directive (AVMSD)

2010

EU

Applies to traditional TV broadcast, on-demand services. Not just an accessibility law.

European Electronics Communications Code

2018

EU

Covers communication through calls, SMS, web-based service, not only accessibility. Requires that services and service information is accessible.

Regulation on electronic identification and trust services (eIDAS)

2014

EU

General regulation governing digital transactions requiring e-Signatures. Includes accessibility.

EU Procurement Directive

2014

EU

All procured services and documentation about those services must meet Technical Specifications, now EN 301 549.

US Federal Acquisition Regulation

1973

US

Implements Section 508 of the Rehabilitation act in Part 39, requires accessibility for service procurement under threat of penalties.

European Web Accessibility Directive

2016

EU

Web accessibility in the public sector using EN 301 549.

European Accessibility Act

2019

EU

Web accessibility in the private sector using EN 301 549.
