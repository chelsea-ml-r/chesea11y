CPACC Study Notes

Introduction

Hi! I am currently in the process of studying for the International Association of Accessibility Professional's Certified Professional in Accessibility Core Competencies exam. I write new notes every day in Notes by referencing the exam's Body of Knowledge and all of its linked sources.

This page is an attempt to organize my notes to be more usable, as I am tackling the material in a non-linear fashion and am constantly distracted and preoccupied by other material, which I also write about alongside my study notes.

So within this blog-within-a-blog, I post just the notes, with the hopes they can help you on your journey. Caution; the entries are not yet checked for grammar and spelling. Proceed at your own risk.
